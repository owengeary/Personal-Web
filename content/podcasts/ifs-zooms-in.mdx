# IFS Zooms In: The Economy

Experts from the Institute for Fiscal Studies (IFS) take a deep dive into the economic issues facing the UK. Hosted by IFS Director Paul Johnson, the podcast brings clarity and evidence to the debate on everything from tax and spending to inequality and growth.
