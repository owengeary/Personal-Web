# Political Currency

Former Chancellor George Osborne and former Shadow Chancellor Ed Balls take you inside the room where decisions are made. These two frenemies debate the biggest political and economic stories of the week, explaining how the world of politics and money really works.

With their combined experience at the top of British politics, they offer unique insights into the inner workings of Westminster and the global economy.
