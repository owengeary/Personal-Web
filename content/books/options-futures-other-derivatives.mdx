# Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives

often referred to as the "bible" of the derivatives market, John Hull's masterpiece is the definitive guide to derivative securities. It provides a deep dive into the mechanics of futures markets, hedging strategies, and the pricing of options using the Black-Scholes-Merton model. The book also explores advanced topics like credit derivatives, interest rate models, and volatility smiles, making it indispensable for students and practitioners alike.
