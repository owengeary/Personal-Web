# Fundamentals of Credit and Credit Analysis

This text provides a comprehensive overview of credit risk management. Ziegel breaks down the essential components of credit analysis, from understanding financial statements to assessing borrower character and capacity. It bridges the gap between theoretical finance and practical lending decisions, making it a staple for anyone involved in corporate banking or credit risk.
